M6 Testing Writeup:

EnemyTakeDamageNotDead: Tests that after combat, if an enemy takes damage that is non-lethal, that it will not be marked for deletion. This ensures that the enemy will NOT disappear in our respective method implementation. 
EnemyTakeDamageDead: Tests that after combat, if an enemy takes damage that is lethal, that it will be marked for deletion. This enssures that the enemy WILL disappear in our respective method implementation.
